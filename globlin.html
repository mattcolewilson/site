Globlin — A Free Logotype

This font began as a pixel font, using a 5×6 grid. But once I rounded the corners, I realized I was onto something more fun and unique. Includes A-Z in all caps (with variations in place of lowercase characters), punctuation, common glyphs, and most accents.
