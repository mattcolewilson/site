About this Website

This is sort of half graphic design portfolio and half personal website. It was coded by hand using (mostly) HTML and CSS. It doesn't contain any tracking code or collect any information about you. No cookies, no analytics, no harvesting of data. It's also designed to load fast and take up (relatively) little bandwidth and space.
