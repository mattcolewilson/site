About the Webmaster

Hi! My name is Matt Cole Wilson (he/him). You can call me Matt. I'm a graphic designer based in the Kansas City area. My specialties include logo design, layout, and typography — and I have a soft spot for print projects. I've been doing graphic design professionally since 2010.

Outside of design, I enjoy playing video games, reading horror novels, surfing the web, and hanging out with my wife and our cat.

I'm currently employed full-time, but am always interested in small freelance projects and collaborations. I also would love to do pro-bono work for progressive causes, non-profit organizations, and personal hobby projects that I find interesting.

Want to work together or chat? Don’t hesitate to reach out!

About this Website

This site was designed to be simple and relatively lightweight as a tiny protest against the bloated modern internet.

It was built using static HTML and CSS. There are no tracking cookies and no analytics. (And the only javascript is in the status.cafe widget.) You are not being watched while you are on my site (as far as I know). And I think that’s nice!
