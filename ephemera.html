Ephemera Zine

In December of 2021, I made a zine called ✦ EPHEMERA ✦ and mailed it to a bunch of friends. It's comprised of graphic design, photos, and a few nods to pop culture. There isn't really a cohesive theme, aside from stuff I like and a handful of graphic design scraps that were laying around. Essentially, it was meant to be a low stakes project — just a quick offloading of things I wanted to put out into the world.

It was printed with black ink on a few different colors of ASTROBRIGHTS paper. I'm super happy with how it came out. I bet it would glow under a blacklight. Need to test.
