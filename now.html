Now

This is a Now Page. A concept seemingly invented by Derek Sivers that's meant to showcase what you're doing right now. Kinda self-explanatory. But a neat idea! I will update this page when I feel like it, but hopefully pretty often.

2024.02.23 – Friday

Design

Finishing up my Famicase design for this year and getting it ready for submission. It's maybe a little derivative but I think it's gonna be my best one yet. Also thinking about redesigning my website ((again)) and all the stuff that’s on it. We’ll see.

Personal

I’m like 90% over a cold, but have been kinda sick for like two weeks now. Shit sucks, dude. Today’s my wife’s birthday. (Happy birthday, Alyson!) But we’ve postponed our weekend plans and a visit from her brother, because we’re both under the weather. Still having a nice day, though.

Recommendations

I’ve recently been inspired by all the cool stuff Jack Grimes and Ravi Maher post on their Are.na boards. (Are.na is extra appealing lately, because the Tumblr CEO has been showing his whole ass.) Poking around on Are.na also led me back to Lauren Gallagher’s website, which is really great and prompted the previously-mentioned desire to redesign my own site.

Playing

Currently playing Cyberpunk 2077. This is my second go and I'm having a nice time overall. Focusing on shotguns and hitting bad guys with a big hammer. Significantly more fun than my generic, jack-of-all-trades first playthrough back in 2021. (And a little less janky, too.)

Reading

I’ve started The Shining Girls by Lauren Beukes a couple of times, which is the February pick of the Last Words Book Club. But it’s just not clicking. Maybe I’m not giving it a fair chance, or maybe I’m just not in the mood. Probably gonna bail. Mexican Gothic is next month’s pick. Aside from that, I’m newly interested in reading Neuromancer by William Gibson for cyberpunk reasons.

Listening

I’ve been poking around at some older, heavy stuff. Recently discovered Godflesh, which I think I’m into. A whole lot of stuff to explore there. I’ve also been listening to the first Kittie album. It kicks ass. I knew of them as a teen, but never really got into them. Some real nostalgic sounds.

Watching

Just finished up Tim Rogers 8-hour, choose-your-own-adventure analysis of Cyberpunk 2077. One of the best to ever do it. And he’s right! The last movie I watched was They Came Together on Valentine’s Day, which is probably the funniest movie of the last 10 years.
