Reading

Just finished reading Mexican Gothic by Silvia Moreno-Garcia for the . I think it’s a little in the “not for me” column, but I get what it’s doing and it does it all very well. Just maybe a little to clean for my tastes? (I dunno, join the book club if you wanna talk about it!) Up next is The Dreamer in Fire by Sam Gafford. Listening

Lately I’ve been obsessively listening to Squeeze by SASAMI. I was a big fan of “Sorry Entertainer” (the incredible Daniel Johnston cover) when it came out two years ago. But for whatever reason it’s taken me this long to give the entire album a listen. But now I’m hooked. My favorite track is “Say It.”

Another recent deep dive is You Want it Darker by Leonard Cohen. A hell of an album. “If I Didn’t Have Your Love” may be the best love song ever written

Watching
