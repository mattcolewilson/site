Now

This is a Now Page. A concept seemingly invented by Derek Sivers that's meant to showcase what you're doing right now. Kinda self-explanatory, but a neat idea! I will update this page when I feel like it, but hopefully pretty often.

2024.05.31 – Friday

Design

Not doing much outside of the day job lately! I’ve been heavily procrastinating on personal projects. Planning to eventually make a bunch of artwork for fictional compact discs. And I still want to make a Blair Witch Project zine. But nothing tangible to speak of at the moment.

Personal

Recently celebrated 13 years of marriage. It’s the best! And I got a new tattoo of some Tom Waits lyrics: “We’re chained to the world and we all gotta pull.” A good line, imo.

I’ve also been stumbling down a rabbit hole of music making stuff. It started by getting into Godflesh and learning about the sounds they’re able to get out of a drum machine. I found a way to program beats using samples from that same drum machine, which has been a lot of fun. And then I stumbled into the free Yamaha DX-7 synthesizer emulator Dexed, which is bonkers. I do not really know what I’m doing, but I’m enjoying my time.

Playing

Thanks to the incredible N64 recompilation project, I’ve been playing The Legend of Zelda: Majora’s Mask. I had Ocarina of Time back in the day, but had never played Majora’s Mask until now. It’s great! The time constraints aren’t as stressful as I thought they’d be and it’s mostly a joy to play. Especially with all the nice features of the recompilation. Highly recommended!

Reading

Finished reading The Only Good Indians by Stephen Graham Jones for the book club. Loved it. A few flaws stick out more on this second read, but I’m still a big fan of SGJ. Also finished Herding Tigers for work. Solid. Up next is Tender is the Flesh by Agustina Bazterrica.

Listening

As mentioned above, I’ve been listening to a lot of Godflesh. My current favorite is Love and Hate in Dub. Also recently discovered Botch. (Saw someone on the internet call Norma Jean a Botch ripoff, lol, so I had to check them out.) A great band! We Are the Romans!

Watching

Watched The Super Mario Bros. Movie and pretty strongly disliked it! I think it’s a bad movie! Also revisited Mars Attacks for the first time since childhood. Pretty fun, but I’m not really a Tim Burton guy. And finally, we watched Madame Web and the reports are true: it’s not great.

Recommendations

Big clouds, ice cream sandwiches, and this post by Jack Grimes about UFOlogy.
