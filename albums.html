New Music to Listen To

It's really easy to only listen to the same few albums you discovered in high school. But there’s a ton of great, new stuff coming out all of the time that deserves your attention. This page is a collection of new music that I fully recommend. Listed from newest to oldest. Only going back a couple of years.

Last updated: July 23, 2026

June 2026 / Rap + Alt Hip Hop / Listen
One of the greatest rappers, imo. Extremely fun wordplay.
