What I've Been Up To

I am writing this shortly after skipping the gym to stay home and eat pizza and play video games. Sometimes it really does feel like I'm living the dream. What's happened since my last post? Not a ton of stuff. Just enjoying the Summer and doing my best to have a good time.

Alyson and I recently played through Split Fiction. The story is a little rough, but the co-op gaming is a great time. Lots of neat gimmicks. Would recommend. I'm currently playing BIRDCAGE, a PS1-esque SHMUP. I'm not very good at it, but the aesthetics are doing a lot to keep me interested. Lots of cool inspirations on display, but the most noteworthy, imo, is Starfighter Sanvein. GOOD TASTE.

Speaking of video games, Alyson and I went to a VR place with a couple of friends last weekend. It was my first time trying VR and I kinda loved it. Maybe I'll get a headset someday in the distant future.

Still trying to do little creative side projects in my spare time. I have recently pivoted from trying to make chiptune to designing fictional movie logos. I don't have enough put together to show yet, but it's a fun exercise. Thinking about movie logos and coming up with fake titles is a lot of fun. Give it a try.
