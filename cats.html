The Cats

This is a webpage dedicated to my two cats. This is a cool and normal thing that I spent time making. They're good cats.

Arcade

We got this guy as a kitten straight off the streets. He was a sweet lap cat initially, but has grown into a little bit of a jerk who's only affectionate for like 10 minutes a day. But even if he likes his personal space, he's still the chillest cat I've ever been around. Doesn't even object to nail trims.

Fun Fact: Named after Arcade Gannon from Fallout: New Vegas.

Rex
