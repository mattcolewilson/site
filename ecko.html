ecko (font design)

This A-Z logotype is based on the logo for Marc Eckō's Getting Up: Contents Under Pressure. The game is a hidden gem, originally released on the PS2, about grafitti. I wanted to find the original logo/font, but after my searches were unsuccessful, I decided to recreate it myself.

Note: I'm not the original designer of the game logo, this is a fan recreation. Use at your own risk!
