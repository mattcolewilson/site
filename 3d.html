Item Render: 3D Models

Back in 2022, I got way into 3D modelling. My goal was to replicate the aesthetic of the PlayStation 1. Almost everything I learned was from theSicklyWizard on YouTube.

It was super fun to dive in and learn how to create low-poly models. I still enjoy watching these 3D object rotate infinitely on a black background. Maybe I'll get back into it someday. In the meantime, here are a few of my favorite pieces.

All models made in Blender. Dithering effect acheived in Photoshop.
